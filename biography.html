Biography

Tenor, Konstantin  Lee (Myonghyun Lee) 

Myonghyun is the recipient of awards from various competitions, including the Korea Voice Competition, where he received first prize. He was awarded 1st  place in the SWR Young Opera Stars and 1st prize at the Neue Stimmen Singing Competition in Germany as well as 1st prize at the Luis Sigall Competition in Chile. He also received 3rd prize at the Montreal International Competition and 4th prize at the Seoul International Competition. He has represented South Korea at the BBC Cardiff Singer of the World.
